An Outdoorsman's Paradise
From mountains and world-class fishing in the north to island hopping and the rolling countryside in the south to Allemansrätten all over, Sweden is the perfect bucketlist destination for outdoor lovers.

Popular Places To Visit

Stockholm

Known for its islands, Abba museum, subway art, cafe culture, and delicious meatballs.

Malmö

Known for its modern architecture, farmer's markets, food scene, historic Malmo Castle, and the longest road/rail bridge in Europe.

Swedish Lapland

Known for the Kungsleden trail, Abisko, the Aurora Borealis, Sami culture, and reindeer.

Umeå

Known for Sami culture, silver birch lined streets, a lively art culture, backcountry skiing, and historic Umeå University.
